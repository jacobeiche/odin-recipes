Thai Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Thai red curry paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 pound chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, snap peas)
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Cooked jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic and Thai red curry paste. Stir for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add the sliced chicken breast and cook until it is no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for another 5 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  6. Stir in the fish sauce and brown sugar. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve hot over cooked jasmine rice.
